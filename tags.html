Background

Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) is a Nigerian entrance examination board for tertiary-level institutions. It is responsible for conducting the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) for prospective undergraduates and the Direct Entry (DE) examination for prospective direct entry candidates.

JAMB Portal

The JAMB portal is an online platform that provides access to various services offered by JAMB. These services include UTME registration, checking of results, printing of admission letters, and more.

JAMB Results Checker

The JAMB result checker is an online platform that allows candidates to check their UTME results. To check your result, visit the JAMB result-checking portal and enter your JAMB registration number or email address in the required column.

JAMB e-Facility

The JAMB Efacility is an online platform that provides access to various services offered by JAMB. These services include UTME registration, checking of results, printing of admission letters, and more.

JAMB Site

The official website of JAMB is https://www.jamb.gov.ng/. This website provides information about JAMB’s activities, services offered by JAMB, news updates, and more.

JAMB Registration

To register for UTME or DE examination, candidates must create an account on the JAMB efacility portal. After creating an account, candidates can proceed to register for the examination.

JAMB Matriculation List

The matriculation list is a list of candidates who have been admitted into various tertiary institutions in Nigeria. It is important that candidates confirm their names from the matriculation list as only candidates whose names are on this list are recognized as bonafide students from the various accredited institutions. By implication, if your name is not on this list, your application to the NYSC scheme for the one-year National service shall be declined.
