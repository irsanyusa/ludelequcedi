Heidi Klum, 50, and Tom Kaulitz, 33, Celebrate Their 4th Anniversary and Prove Love Knows No Age

Publish date: 2024-08-27

The famous relationship between this rocker and model has often made headlines. The couple has a 17-year age difference, and with each year, their relationship only gets stronger. Heidi posted a series of photos on Instagram, and the couple looks happier than ever.

How a model and a Tokio Hotel guitarist met each other.

Heidi and Tom met in Germany while she was hosting Germany’s Next Top Model. They were both divorced and had a lot of things in common. They started dating in 2018 and shortly afterward secretly tied the knot in February 2019. The model recollects, “I met Bill Kaulitz, his twin brother, many years ago, but I didn’t know he had a twin brother that I was going to marry.”

They got hitched in Italy.

Heidi commented on her wedding plans, “We’re 2 Germans, it will be very organized and on time. Very, very much on time and very organized.” The couple happily said, “I do” in Capri, Italy. The celebration took place on a luxury yacht called the Christina O. The model later posted a photo on Instagram showing their stunning wedding and her fabulous floral dress.

Heidi divorced twice before she met Tom.

Her first husband was a stylist Ric Pipino. They spent 5 years together, then, for some reason, decided to divorce. Her second husband was Seal. Both felt an instant spark, but with time it faded away.

The announcement shocked the public, and Heidi explained, “We’re in the public, so the highs are out there. I don’t think it’s necessary, especially for our children, to have the lows being printed in magazines and talked about.”

Now it’s Tom and Heidi’s 4th anniversary.

The couple celebrated their romance on August 3. She shared several photos from the trip, and it’s obvious that their chemistry is still as strong as when they first met each other. The star wrote in German, “Ich bin soooo verliebt in Dich Tom Kaulitz,” which translates to, “I am so in love with you Tom Kaulitz.”

Their 17-year age gap is not an obstacle.

Many have criticized them and Heidi responded, “A lot of people weren’t giving us positive vibes when we started dating. If it feels right when you’re at home, and you know what you have together, that’s the most important thing. Cut out all the noise. Not everyone is cheering you on in all things that you do. Do what makes you happy. He made me happy. We’re still having a great time together.”
