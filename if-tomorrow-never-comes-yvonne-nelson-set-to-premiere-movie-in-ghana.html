Yvonne Nelson set to premiere movie in Ghana

Publish date: 2024-08-21

The talented and award winning filmmaker recently shared some undefined and from her latest movie project

Yvonne took to her Instagram page to announce that the movie will be premiering in Ghana on June 5, 2015 at Accra and west hills malls.

'If Tomorrow Never Comes' stars Yvonne Nelson, Deyemi Okalawon, Becca, Ghanaian boxer, Ayittey Powers, Bismark Nii Odoiamong other talented actors.
