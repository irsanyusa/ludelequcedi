America’s Got Talent: The Champions star Angelina Jordan has released a new six-song EP called Old Enough, featuring her previously released song “Love Don’t Let Me Go.” The singer also shared a music video for the song “Now I’m the Fool.”

Angelina Jordan Releases New EP Old Enough

The music video for Angelina’s new song “Now I’m the Fool” has a similar vibe to her video for “Love Don’t Let Me Go.” The singer sports a ’60s-style hairdo as she performs at a microphone in the black and white clip.

Angelina is definitely channeling artists like Amy Winehouse and Adele on her new music, while bringing her own signature sound to the tracks. The 17-year-old’s husky voice brought her to the Finals of AGT: The Champions in 2020.

The EP also features an uplifting song called “Fire and Rain,” in which Angelina sings that she’s “getting stronger every day.” The track “All My Fault” finds her expressing regrets about a relationship. On the soulful “Good in Goodbyes,” Angelina finds the positives in a breakup.

The EP ends with the fierce song “Diamond,” in which Angelina sings that she’s “shining like a supernova.”

Angelina Earned an AGT Golden Buzzer

Angelina got her start on Norway’s Got Talent, which she won at the age of 8. At age 13, she auditioned for the second season of AGT: The Champions, wowing the judges with her rendition of Queen’s “Bohemian Rhapsody.”

The performance earned a Golden Buzzer from judge Heidi Klum, sending Angelina straight to the Finals, where she performed “Goodbye Yellow Brick Road” by Elton John. Unfortunately, she didn’t earn enough Superfan votes to make it into the Top 5.

However, she’s continued to release music since her time on the show. In 2021, she debuted the original single “7th Heaven.” Now fans have this new EP to enjoy from the talented young singer.
