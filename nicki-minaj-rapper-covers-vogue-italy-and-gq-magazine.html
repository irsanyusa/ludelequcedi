Rapper covers Vogue Italy and GQ magazine

Publish date: 2024-09-13

For the month of December alone, she has grazed two very reputable magazines in the world, Vogue Italia and GQ.

For GQ Magazine, which is targeted at guys, the magazine christened 2014 "The year of the butt" and it's only expected that Nicki Minaj would be on such a list.

Super bass singer is seen looking super classy with an elaborate hair do for her photoshoot with Vogue Italia. The Nicki Minaj Vogue shoot was styled by Rushka Bergman, shot by Francesco Carrozzini , makeup done by Mylah Morales and the hair magic was performed by hair stylist called Brent Lawler.
