Nigerian pastor Oba Solomon clears the air over his invitation by the police

Publish date: 2024-05-26

Oba Solomon on Wednesday, February 2, 2022 visited the Zone 2 Police Command, Onikan Lagos to upon invitation by the police.

After his visit, the preacher told this reporter that he was invited over something he had said about the Koran.

Known for some controversial takes in his social media preacher, the pastor said he was invited to broker peace with the Muslim committee.

"I did not commit any crime, there is no any problem; the reason they called me is that they called for peace between a brother and a sister," he said.

"The reason they called me is because of love and we have settled everything."

"I was not fighting with the person, It was because of what I preached about Koran and the bible," he also said.

"The major one is because of the Koran so Muslims called for the meeting today so they won't be any disagreement between two religions."

He also had good things to say about the Nigerian police and said he was treated with respect during the meeting.

"The police are very responsible. They respect me a lot and I respect them, so I use this opportunity to tell all Nigerians that policemen are our friend," he said.

He however refused to back down from speaking on issues although he said he would avoid things that might cause trouble.

"Nobody can stop me from saying the truth but all that I know would cause any problem, I will adjust," he said.
