Mavins drop new single 'Won Da Mo' off upcoming compilation album

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Song Title: Won Da Mo

Genre: Amapiano

Date of Release: November 9th, 2022

ADVERTISEMENT

Producer: Andre Vibez

Length: 4 minutes 07 seconds

Features: NONE

ADVERTISEMENT

Label: Mavins Global Holdings/Platoon

Details/Takeaway: Mavin Records line up combine for an Amapiano record set to thrill listeners and build up anticipation for the release of their upcoming compilation project.

Rema starts the head banging song steaming words of gratitude on the energetic beat which also sees him embark on a subtle braggado. Crayon, delivers an unforgettable electric hook with Boy Spyce adding a sonic seasoning on the record with his cocky verse. Bayanni hops on the song with an unexpected audaciousness which paves way for Magixx’s grandeur on the record. With a rhythmic speech that commands attention, Ladipoe delivers his bit with crisp precision ushering Ayra Starr on the record where she delivers her punchline filled verse with her silvery voice. Johnny Drille brings the song to a soothing end with his unexpected yet memorable verse.
