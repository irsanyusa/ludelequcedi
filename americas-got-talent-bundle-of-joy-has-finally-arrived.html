America’s Got Talent fan favorite Us The Duo officially announced the arrival of their baby girl Xyla Rose Alvarado.

‘Us the baby’ was born on October 8th at 1:28 p.m. weighing 7lbs and 6 ounces, at 19 inches long. The duo took to Instagram to express their excitement saying, “In 24 hours, she has already changed our life for the better and we can’t wait for you to meet her someday.”

Looking back on the AGT quarterfinals, the duo performed an emotionally charged original song dedicated to their baby girl titled Like I Did With You. “Tonight we are singing a song about our baby. The song talks about me wanting the baby to be just like her because I know as soon as I hold the baby in my arms and I’m going to look into her eyes and fall in love with her just like I did with her mama,” Michael said.

Prior to motherhood Carissa told PEOPLE magazine, “Hearing the heartbeat for the first time, oh my gosh, as musician’s we get to hear so many beautiful sounds but by far that is my favorite sound that I’ve ever heard.”

“Having a baby come into this world changes the way I think about things,” said new father Michael. “Being a musician definitely has ups and downs financially, you never know when a paycheck is going to come.”
