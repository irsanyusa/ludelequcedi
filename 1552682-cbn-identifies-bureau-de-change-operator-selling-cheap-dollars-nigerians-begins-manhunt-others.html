Too Cheap&quot;: CBN Names BDC Operator Selling Dollars Below N700/$, Begins Manhunt for Others

Publish date: 2024-09-15
  • The Central Bank of Nigeria is investigating a Bureau de Change operator for selling dollars cheaper than the official rate
  • The move is part of ongoing efforts to stabilise the forex market and combat illegal financial transactions
  • There are over 5,000 Bureau de Change operators in Nigeria licensed by the CBN to buy and sell dollars

The Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) is investigating a Crown Agent, a Bureau De Change (BDC) operator, for selling dollars below the official rate.

Data from FMDQ securities shows that at the official market's Investors and Exporters window, the naira closed at N744.97/$1 on Tuesday, September 5, 2023.

The concern by CBN means that the BDC operator must have been selling the forex below N700 per dollar, which is considered too cheap.
