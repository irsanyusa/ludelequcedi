Oh, Canada! The WWE is going “up north” with Money In The Bank 2024. This year’s pro wrestling event features five matchups — including two title bouts for the World Heavyweight Championship and Intercontinental Championship — on Saturday, July 6.

For the main event, WWE World Heavyweight Champion Damian Priest is set to defend the belt against Seth “Freakin” Rollins in a high-stakes ladder match.

When Is WWE Money In The Bank 2024? Date, Time

WWE Money In The Bank takes place at Scotiabank Arena in Toronto in Ontario, Canada on Saturday, July 6. The start time for WWE Money In The Bank is 7 p.m. ET/4 p.m. PT.
