The Best Celebrity Halloween Costumes of 2023

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Hollywood’s best dressed up over the weekend to celebrate the spooky season ahead of Halloween on Tuesday.

Celebrities gathered at the annual Casamigos Halloween party on Oct. 27 in Beverly Hills. The star-studded guest list included Kaia Gerber and Austin Butler, Paris Hilton, Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly, Justin Bieber, Rande Gerber and Cindy Crawford, Jessica Alba, Victoria Justice, Jodie Turner-Smith and Tyga, among others.

Meanwhile, some celebrities took to social media to share their looks, including Chloe Bailey, who sported long blonde locks and a metallic body suit to transform into Daenerys Targaryen from “Game of Thrones.”

Take a look at some of the best costumes celebrities are wearing this Halloween:

  • Kaia Gerber and Austin Butler

    The power couple as artist’s muse and artist: Kaia Gerber as Warhol superstar Edie Sedgwick and Austin Butler as Andy Warhol. 

  • Paris Hilton

    “You’re toxic, I’m slippin’ under!” — Paris Hilton decked out as friend Britney Spears. 

  • Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly

    Megan Fox as Gogo Yubari and Machine Gun Kelly as Beatrix Kiddo from “Kill Bill.” 

  • Victoria Justice

    The “Victorious” star honored music icon Cher. 

  • Rande Gerber and Cindy Crawford

    A “Grease”-inspired couple’s pairing with Rande Gerber as Danny Zuko and Cindy Crawford as Sandy Olsson. 

  • Kelly Sawyer Patricof and Jessica Alba

    Paris Hilton wasn’t the only one inspired by Britney Spears. Kelly Sawyer Patricof took on the Britney from “Hit Me Baby One More Time,” while Jessica Alba bedazzled with her “Toxic”-inspired outfit. 

  • Justin Bieber and Presley Gerber

    Justin Bieber and Presley Gerber were dressed as…some guys on vacation?

  • Tyga and Evan Ross

    Tyga truly “terrified” as Art the Clown from the “Terrifier” franchise. On the other hand, Evan Ross took it more lowkey, looking like another version of himself. 

  • Liza Koshy

    Liza Koshy is ready to wrap chocolates as Lucy Ricardo from “I Love Lucy.”

  • Jenna Dewan and Steve Kazee

    Jenna Dewan as Winona Ryder’s Kim Boggs and Steve Kazee as Edward Scissorhands.

  • Rachel Lindsay

    There’s arguably no better version of Rihanna than Superbowl Rihanna. 

  • Jodie Turner-Smith and Nancy Gomez

    Naughtier takes on the schoolgirl and nun for Turner-Smith and Gomez, respectively. 

  • Chord Overstreet and Glen Powell

    Chord Overstreet and Glen Powell start their engines as Cal Naughton Jr. and Ricky Bobby from “Talladega Nights: The Legend of Ricky Bobby.”
