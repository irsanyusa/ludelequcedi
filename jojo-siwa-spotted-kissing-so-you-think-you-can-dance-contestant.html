It looks like So You Think You Can Dance judge JoJo Siwa might be in a new relationship. Siwa was recently spotted locking lips with Madison Rouge Alvarado, a former contestant on SYTYCD Season 18.

JoJo Siwa Seen Kissing SYTYCD Contestant

According to Entertainment Tonight, Siwa was photographed kissing Alvarado at LAX last week. Alvarado can be seen holding Siwa’s face in her hands as they share a smooch next to a luggage cart. The pair met while filming the most recent season of SYTYCD.

Siwa served as a judge for the later episodes of the season, as Alvarado made it to the Top 3 but ultimately lost to winner Anthony Curley. In a clip shared by Alvarado on Instagram, Siwa can be heard praising her talent.

“In between takes you were in pain. But during those takes you would have never known,” Siwa told the dancer. “Also, in So You Think You Can Dance, it is very rare for someone to go the entire season without being in the bottom, and you have done that.”

What Do Fans Think of the Possible Romance?

ET posted the photo of Siwa and Alvarado on Instagram, and the comments were a mixed bag. One user commented, “they’re both hot.” However, another wrote, “I honestly cannot think of anything that I care about less than this.”

Some even speculated that the apparent romance might not even be real, with one person writing, “Oh god how much did JoJo’s agent have to pay this poor girl for this publicity stunt.”

Many of the comments were negative. Still, others defended Siwa against the haters, with one user commenting, “She deserves to be happy and live her life. I’m so happy for her to find somebody to love and truly support her.”
