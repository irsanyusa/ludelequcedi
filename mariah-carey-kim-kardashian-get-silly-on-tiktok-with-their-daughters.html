Mariah Carey and Kim Kardashian recently hung out together with their daughters, Monroe Cannon and North West. The four of them even made a cute TikTok video together lip syncing to Carey’s song “It’s a Wrap.”

Mariah Carey, Kim Kardashian Make TikTok Video Together

The new TikTok video shows North and Monroe dancing and lip syncing to Carey’s 2009 song “It’s a Wrap.” After a few seconds, their moms poke their heads in from out of frame, lip syncing into hairbrushes.

North and Monroe then jokingly push their moms away before the video ends. Both Kardashian and Carey shared the video on their accounts, writing in the caption, “It’s a wrap! But never for us!”

Some commenters couldn’t believe that Kardashian and Carey were hanging out together. As one fan wrote in the comments of Kardashian’s video, “imagine casually inviting Mariah Carey to your house.”

It looks like Monroe and North have formed a friendship of their own. Kardashian’s joint account with North features a video of the 9-year-old dancing along to Rihanna’s “Work” while 11-year-old Monroe lip syncs. It appears to be a recreation of Rihanna’s recent Super Bowl performance.

Carey Performed with Her Daughter for Christmas

This isn’t the first time Carey has shared a performance with her daughter Monroe. In December of last year, Monroe joined her mom on stage for the singer’s first live show since the start of the Covid-19 pandemic.

Carey and Monroe performed “Away in a Manger” together, and Monroe showed off a beautiful singing voice. Carey told the crowd that the day Monroe and her twin brother Moroccan were born was “the greatest gift ever.”

The singer shares the twins with her ex-husband Nick Cannon. Kardashian, meanwhile, shares four kids with her ex-husband Kanye West — 9-year-old North, 7-year-old Saint, 5-year-old Chicago, and 3-year-old Psalm.
