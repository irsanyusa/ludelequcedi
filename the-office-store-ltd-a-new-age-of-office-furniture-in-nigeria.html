The Office Store Ltd: A new age of office furniture in Nigeria

Publish date: 2024-07-08

You can’t miss it, they have a road side display in front of the main showroom that is constantly being refreshed in touch with the latest trends in Office Furniture.

Pulse Nigeria

The Office Store is one of the largest wholesalers of office chairs for 15 years now and recently started woodworking in 2016. They are a young company constantly introducing new products to the Nigerian market like standing tables, wireless charging tables as well as introducing the mesh back chair to Nigeria in 2011.

ADVERTISEMENT

They manufacture wooden furniture as well as metal furniture like bunk beds and chair legs, all using machines from Europe.

Before entering the store you will notice a wide array of parking options meaning you are free to go in and out without having to walk to or wait for your car.

As you enter the store you are greeted by a sign saying “PROUDLY MANUFACTURED IN NIGERIA” which is stunning as the quality of the finishing of the furniture rivals that of what you find in London.
