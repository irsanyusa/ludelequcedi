Full List of Nominees Revealed

Publish date: 2024-09-03

The nominations for the 63d TV Week Logie Awards have been announced, including the seven television personalities competing for the Gold Logie Award for Most Popular Personality on Australian Television.

In the running for the top going are previous winner Hamish Blake, presenter and comedian Julia Morris, presenter and journalist Leigh Sales, actor Mark Cole Smith, presenter and broadcaster Osher Günsberg, presenter and comedian Shaun Micallef, and entertainer and presenter Sonia Kruger.

This year’s awards ceremony will make the move from Queensland’s Gold Coast to Sydney. They will take place at The Star Sydney on Sunday, July 30.

Voting for the public-voted categories – including the Gold Logie, Bert Newton Award for Most Popular Presenter, Most Popular Actor, Most Popular Actress, Most Popular New Talent, Most Popular Drama Series, Miniseries or Telemovie, Most Popular Entertainment Program, Most Popular Current Affairs Program, Most Popular Comedy Program, Most Popular Reality Program, and Most Popular Lifestyle Program – is open now and will close on the day of the ceremony.

Gold Logie voting will remain open throughout the telecast until 10:30 p.m. (AEST).

A further 12 categories, dubbed “Most Outstanding”, rather than “Most Popular”, are determined by a panel of industry experts and insiders.

This year, a limited number of red-carpet bleacher seats and ceremony seats will go on sale to the general public via Ticketek.

The ceremony will be broadcast on Channel 7 and 7Plus, led by comedian and host Sam Pang.
