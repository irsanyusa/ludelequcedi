What the Cast of Squid Game Looks Like in Real Life

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Squid Game has instantly turned into a global phenomenon by putting all of us on the edge of our seats. This South Korean series has hooked us on its suspenseful plot, captivating cinematography, and of course relatable characters with a cast of very different, but equally fascinating roles that got us emotionally invested right away. This is why we were super curious to get to know the real people standing behind these powerfully portrayed personas.

Bright Side did some digging to bring you the people hiding behind some of your favorite characters. For some, you will be really surprised by the contrast between the actor and the character they played.

1. Lee Jung-jae as Seong Gi-hun

2. HoYeon Jung as Kang Sae-byeok

3. Park Hae-soo as Cho Sang-woo

4. Wi Ha-Joon as Hwang Jun-ho

5. Lee Byung-hun as Front man

6. Heo Sung-tae as Jang Deok-su

7. Kim Joo-Ryoung as Han Mi-nyeo

8. Anupam Tripathi as Ali Abdul

9. Lee Yoo-mi as Ji-yeong

10. Gong Yoo as the Salesman
