Kristy Sellars Announces Upcoming TV Performance, Missouri Residency

Publish date: 2024-07-08

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Casey Durkin/NBC

Reading Time: 3 minutes

America’s Got Talent alum Kristy Sellars is now gearing up for her upcoming gigs. The pole dancing superstar recently announced that she’ll be performing on a television show and having her own full show in Missouri, United States.

Kristy Sellars Reveals “2 Exciting Announcements”

In an Instagram video posted last week, Sellars took the time to update her followers about her upcoming gigs. The pole dancer was in a PhysiPole Studio and wearing her practice shirt and jacket.

:I am back in the studio after a couple of months of very light training slash rest that I am now paying for because I started training like 48 hours ago and I’m in so much pain,” she said.

Despite complaining about her body pain, Sellars nonetheless said it’s been good, recognizing that it’s all “part of the process.” She went on and proceeded to reveal her exciting announcement.

“I have two exciting things to tell you about in this video,” she said. “And the first one is that in October slash November this year, I’m going to be performing on a tv show. I can’t tell you what it is yet but I will take you along the behind the scenes journey of me training and preparing and doing all the things that I share with you guys on my channel.”

Subsequently, Sellars stated that a lot of people had been asking her if she’ll be doing a full show. All smiles, she then announced that she’ll be having her own show in the United States next year.

“I’m so excited about it because it’s my act as a full experience, as a full show. You can come and see it at Silver Dollar City in Missouri, United States in April 2024,” she said.

Like what she’ll be doing prior to her television show performance, Sellars plans to bring her fans along her preparation journey. Clearly, the AGT standout is in high spirits for her performances in the future.

Fans Congratulate Her for Her Milestones

Upon hearing about her announcement, her followers were quick to express their support for Sellars. According to them, they can’t wait to watch her on tv and attend her show at Silver Dollar City.  

“So excited for this announcement. Looking forward to seeing you in person in April!” one fan wrote.

“Congratulations!!! You deserve all of this!! You’re so talented and entertaining and so unique,” another user commented.

“Congratulations Kristy!! Such amazing news. Can’t wait to follow you on both journeys,” another fan wrote.

Just recently, Sellars posted a training video of Instagram. As she stated in the video, she’s “definitely stronger and fitter” than she was before.
